Finishing Options

Premium Craftsmanship. Exceptional Identity.

The perfect finishing touch transforms your packaging into a powerful brand statement. Select from our range of high-end techniques to create a lasting first impression.

✨

Gold / Silver Foil Stamping

Ideal for: Luxury Packaging, Jewelry, Cosmetics

Metallic foil is pressed onto the packaging surface using heat and pressure, creating a premium reflective logo or design. Available in gold, silver, rose gold, holographic, and other custom finishes to enhance brand recognition.

🖐️

Soft Touch Lamination

Ideal for: Beauty, Fashion, Premium Consumer Brands

A smooth velvet-like coating that delivers a luxurious tactile experience. Reduces glare while enriching color depth. Often paired with foil stamping or Spot UV to create a sophisticated contrast effect.

💎

Spot UV Coating

Ideal for: Corporate Packaging, Electronics, Premium Retail

A glossy coating applied to selected areas of the design, highlighting logos, graphics, or patterns. Creates visual depth and a premium look while adding a subtle tactile effect.

💼

Embossing / Debossing

Ideal for: Stationery, Candles, Lifestyle Products

Create raised or recessed logo effects directly on the packaging surface. This technique adds texture and dimension without additional ink, delivering a refined and professional appearance.

🌈

Full CMYK Printing

Ideal for: E-commerce, Subscription Boxes, Food Packaging

High-resolution full-color printing for vibrant branding and product presentation. Suitable for both exterior and interior printing, with Pantone color matching available upon request.

🎗️

Custom Inserts & Interior Linings

Ideal for: Jewelry, Watches, Electronics, Perfume

Custom inserts made from EVA foam, velvet, satin, paperboard, or other materials to securely hold products in place. Designed to enhance protection, presentation, and the overall unboxing experience.
