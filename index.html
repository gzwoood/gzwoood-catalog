About Woood Packaging

Your Hassle-Free Packaging Expert

Guangzhou Woood Trading Co., Ltd. is a custom packaging manufacturer based in Guangzhou, China. With over 10 years of industry experience, we specialize in paper packaging solutions, including rigid gift boxes, magnetic folding boxes, corrugated mailer boxes, paper bags, stickers, tissue paper, and other supporting packaging materials.

We help brands create packaging that is practical, attractive, and cost-effective. Customers can share product photos, dimensions, quantity, and design requirements, and our team will provide a detailed quotation and professional suggestions within 24 hours.

Whether you are developing a new packaging project or looking to optimize your current packaging cost, Woood Packaging is ready to support you from concept to delivery.
