Finishing Options

Premium Craftsmanship. Exceptional Identity.

The perfect finishing touch transforms your packaging into a powerful brand statement. Select from our range of high-end techniques to create a lasting first impression.

✨

Gold / Silver Foil Stamping

Ideal for: Luxury Packaging, Jewelry, Cosmetics

Metallic foil is pressed onto the packaging surface using heat and pressure, creating a premium reflective logo or design. Available in gold, silver, rose gold, holographic, and other custom finishes to enhance brand recognition.

🖐️

Soft Touch Lamination

Ideal for: Beauty, Fashion, Premium Consumer Brands

A smooth velvet-like coating that delivers a luxurious tactile experience. Reduces glare while enriching color depth. Often paired with foil stamping or Spot UV to create a sophisticated contrast effect.

💎

Spot UV Coating

Ideal for: Corporate Packaging, Electronics, Premium Retail

A glossy coating applied to selected areas of the design, highlighting logos, graphics, or patterns. Creates visual depth and a premium look while adding a subtle tactile effect.

💼

Embossing / Debossing

Ideal for: Stationery, Candles, Lifestyle Products

Create raised or recessed logo effects directly on the packaging surface. This technique adds texture and dimension without additional ink, delivering a refined and professional appearance.

🌈

Full CMYK Printing

Ideal for: E-commerce, Subscription Boxes, Food Packaging

High-resolution full-color printing for vibrant branding and product presentation. Suitable for both exterior and interior printing, with Pantone color matching available upon request.

🎗️

Custom Inserts & Interior Linings

Ideal for: Jewelry, Watches, Electronics, Perfume

Custom inserts made from EVA foam, velvet, satin, paperboard, or other materials to securely hold products in place. Designed to enhance protection, presentation, and the overall unboxing experience.

The Art of Unboxing: Why Magnetic Closure Boxes are the Gold Standard for Luxury Brands

In the world of high-end retail, the product is only half the story. The other half is the unboxing experience. Magnetic closure boxes have emerged as the "Gold Standard" for luxury packaging, offering a unique combination of structural integrity and psychological satisfaction.

At gzwoood, we've manufactured millions of these units for perfume, jewelry, and high-end electronics. Here is why your brand should consider the "Magnetic Upgrade."

1. The "Click" of Quality

There is a distinct audible "click" when a magnetic box closes. This tactile feedback communicates high quality to the customer instantly. It signals that the contents are precious and securely protected.

2. Reusability and Brand Longevity

Unlike standard cardboard boxes that are often discarded, magnetic rigid boxes are so durable and beautiful that customers tend to keep them. This turns your packaging into a permanent piece of brand furniture in their home.

3. Unlimited Customization

Magnetic boxes offer the most versatile canvas for luxury finishes:

  • Finishes: Soft-touch lamination, anti-scratch coating, or textured linen paper.
  • Embellishments: Gold/Silver foil stamping, Spot UV, or deep embossing for a 3D feel.
  • Inserts: Custom EVA foam, satin linings, or eco-friendly pulp trays.

How to Choose the Ultimate Padel Racket Packaging: A Guide for Premium Sports Brands (2026)

With the explosive growth of Padel in Europe, the USA, and the Middle East, brands are facing a new challenge: How to stand out in a crowded market through packaging?

For professional racket brands, packaging is no longer just a box. It’s the first impression of your brand and the "armor" for your product during global transit. At Guangzhou Woood (gzwoood), we specialize in high-end sports packaging. Here are the top 3 solutions favored by premium brands in 2026.

1. E-commerce Ready: The High-Strength Corrugated Solution

If your business focuses on online sales and global DDP shipping, a high-strength Corrugated Mailer Box is your best choice.

  • Core Advantage: Lightweight, shock-resistant, and cost-effective.
  • Design Tip: Use E-flute material and consider inside printing to tell your brand story during the unboxing experience.

2. The Luxury Statement: Magnetic Rigid Gift Boxes

For retail display or limited edition rackets, a Magnetic Rigid Box is the ultimate symbol of luxury.

  • Core Advantage: Secure magnetic closure, premium soft-touch finish, and exceptional durability.
  • Design Tip: Combine Spot UV, Gold Foiling, or Embossing to make your brand feel like a luxury piece the moment it's held.

3. The Elite Protection: Precision Foam Inserts

The success of packaging often depends on the protection inside.

  • Core Advantage: Custom-cut high-density EVA foam or sustainable paper inserts.
  • Design Tip: 1:1 mold-cutting ensures the racket remains static during transit and can include extra space for balls or accessories.

Why Sustainability Matters?

Overseas markets, especially in Europe and North America, have reached an all-time high demand for sustainable packaging. As an FSC-certified factory, gzwoood’s Padel solutions are 100% recyclable, helping your brand meet international environmental regulations.
